# FPS Coaching Tips Knowledge Base

## Crosshair and Aim

- Keep crosshair at likely head/chest height before peeking.
- Pre-aim common angles instead of wide swinging every corner.
- Fire in controlled bursts when recoil becomes unstable.

## Positioning

- Prefer cover that allows quick re-peek and retreat.
- Avoid standing in open lanes after taking first contact.
- Reposition after revealing your location with repeated shots.

## Decision-Making

- Trade low-percentage fights for better angles and timing.
- If accuracy drops in a duel, break line of sight and reset.
- Prioritize survival and information over unnecessary duels.
- Push a low-health opponent only when you have a health or positional advantage.
- When low health and visible, break line of sight before deciding whether to re-peek.
- Hold cover when the opponent's location or health advantage is uncertain.

## Practice Focus

- Isolate one skill per block (tracking, peeking, recoil control).
- Review one mistake pattern per round and set a correction target.
- Use measurable goals (hit rate, first-shot accuracy, survival time).
